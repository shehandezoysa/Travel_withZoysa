Our Background




Who we are & What we do?


Travel with Zoysa is a family-owned tour operator and travel agency located
in Kalutara, Sri Lanka. We specialize in tours to Sri Lanka but are always
expanding and enhancing our wide range of offers, as well as adding new
and exciting tours of India, Pakistan, China etc.A family-owned business
based in Kalutara,travel with Zoysa was founded in 2016 by Zoysa Brothers
and since that humble beginning it has grown into a trusted resource that
has helped tens of thousands of people better enjoy their travel.
